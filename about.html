About Impact Decor Ltd

Innovation. Integrity. Impact.

Our Story

At Impact Decor Ltd, we bring over 17 years of hands-on experience delivering excellence in transforming homes and spaces with precision, creativity, and care. In the decorative and renovation industry, we have built a reputation for delivering work that stands out for its quality, durability, and attention to detail.

Founded on the principles of Innovation, Integrity, and Impact, we believe that every project, big or small, deserves the highest level of craftsmanship and professionalism. From interior and exterior painting to wallpapering, coving, tiling, flooring, plumbing, plastering, and property refurbishment, our approach remains the same: to enhance your space and exceed your expectations.
